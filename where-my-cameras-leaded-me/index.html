My first night and lightning images.

Digital Camera. My

Cars were another reason I bought my first

I always love to learn so it was not long after
that I bought
my first on
and off camera
flash

journey. Alot changed

When I find a subject that I am interested in I
can not help

Cars became my love as I explored more and
more
possibilities with
a camera.

As I created more images of cars I had the
opportunity to
work with
some incredible
people at Rat Rod Magazine
and
some
others.
Being published was a huge
accomplishment in my life. Now with
multiple covers,
features and write
ups, I am
a
proud photographer.

I have also had the great pleasure of creating
some very
fun portraits
over the years.
Families, kids, seniors,
weddings
and other
forms that

We take trips in our RV for family vacations

an
